    Our Primary Med Spa Services In Tempe, AZ

    Megalift Sculpting

    Leveraging the science of anti-aging, we target all layers of the skin: deep cleansing and exfoliation for the surface layer, radio frequency to stimulate collagen in the middle layer, and microcurrent to tone the underlying facial muscles. These treatments are complemented by LED red light therapy.

    Microneedling

    This med spa reduces scars, shrinks pores, and improves collagen, which will help you fix the damage from UV rays, sun exposure, fine lines, and rough skin.

    Hair Restoration

    Tempe's dry weather can impact scalp health and hair strength. Our holistic hair restoration treatments work both topically and internally to improve scalp hydration.

    RF Collagen Treatment

    Our RF Collagen Treatment boosts your skin’s natural collagen production by warming the deeper dermal layers. This helps tighten lax skin, soften wrinkles, and restore youthful elasticity. You’ll see firmer, smoother skin over time with no recovery time needed.
